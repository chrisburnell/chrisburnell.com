## ghoti

<dl>
    <dt>Pronounced like:</dt>
    <dd><a href="https://dictionary.cambridge.org/pronunciation/english/fish" rel="external noopener">fish</a></dd>
    <dt>Because:</dt>
    <dd>
        tou<u>GH</u> → <strong>f</strong>
        <br>

    </dd>
